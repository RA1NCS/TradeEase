What is Tradease?

Initially, TradeEase was conceptualized as a Trading Script designed to seamlessly integrate with TradingView's Pine Editor. However, as we moved forward, we decided to switch gears a bit. Rather than having the script on the Pine Editor, we thought it would be more user-friendly to host the script ourselves and enable its calls to be received directly on Discord. This way, our users can conveniently receive trading signals without needing to navigate through different platforms!

How do you use it?

It's pretty straightforward to get started! Simply join our Discord server and head over to the #beginner-zone channel. From there, you just need to keep an eye on the signals provided by our bot. Follow these prompts, and you'll be on your way!
